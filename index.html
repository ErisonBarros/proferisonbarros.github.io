Maurício Vancine

Ecologist and Doctoral Student

São Paulo State University (UNESP)

I am a Ecologist and Doctoral Student at the Department of Biodiversity of the São Paulo State University, Rio Claro, Brazil. I have experience in spatial ecology, landscape ecology, quantitative ecology, species distribution modeling (SDMs), amphibian ecology, statistics, and geocomputation
